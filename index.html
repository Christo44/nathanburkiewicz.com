Hello, I'm Nathan.

Developer of the JavaScript kind, designer, husband and the occasional baker striving to be a decent human being and make rad stuff on the internets.

I work as the digital, print and web designer for Yes - Your English Supplement, a monthly magazine for EFL learners and teachers.

While I have dedicated the last 15 years to the EFL and publishing worlds, I now devote my 'free' time to pursuing a career in web development.

I'm an autodidactic JavaScript developer living in Madrid, Spain. Currently I am working my way through Free Code Camp in order to improve my web development skills in general and my JavaScript skills in particular.

Through continuous learning, my aim is to be able to work confidently on both client- and server-side code.

I'm curious by nature, enjoy challenges and consider myself to be a quick learner. As regards work ethic, I strongly believe in the adage, "If you are going to do something, do it well".

If you are developing an exciting project and believe my skills and knowledge could be beneficial, I would love to hear from you.
